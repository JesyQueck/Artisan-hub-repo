    Our Featured Artisans

    Lila Thompson

    A potter from Asheville, NC, Lila specializes in wheel-thrown stoneware with vibrant glazes, inspired by nature.

    Raj Patel

    Raj is a Portland-based woodworker known for intricate carvings and bespoke furniture made from reclaimed wood.

    Sofia Martinez

    A San Francisco textile artist, Sofia creates unique clothing and decor using sustainable materials and traditional techniques.

    Ethan Chen

    Seattle sculptor Ethan specializes in large-scale metal sculptures inspired by urban landscapes.

    Maya Johnson

    Maya, based in Boulder, CO, crafts organic soaps and skincare products using locally sourced ingredients.

    Jamal Robinson

    New Orleans luthier Jamal creates custom guitars that blend traditional craftsmanship with modern sound.
